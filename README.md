# Cactus

A Java-based implementation of a modified Cactus card game.

## Rules
- Goal: Have your four hidden cards equal to the value of 5 or less after you or another player has called "Cactus!".
- Point system:
  - Ace = 1 point
  - King = 0 points
  - Queen = 10 points
  - Jack = 10 points
  - Other cards = pip value
- Special cards:
  - 8 - Allows player to look at one their cards.
  - Jack - Allows player to swap one of their cards with any other player's cards. No looking is allowed.
